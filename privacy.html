Second Brain Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Second Brain handles user information.

Overview

Second Brain is designed to help users manage everyday life admin tasks using step-by-step guides. The app does not collect personal data.

Your privacy is important to us. This policy describes what information the app accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored.

Data Collection

Second Brain does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No personal information is collected
  • No account or sign-in is required
  • No analytics or usage tracking

Data Storage

All tasks and information you create in the app are stored locally on your device using Apple's CoreData framework.

  • Data is stored only on your device
  • Data is never transmitted to external servers
  • Deleting the app removes all associated data

Third-Party Services

Second Brain does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

  • No third-party analytics
  • No data shared with external services
  • No advertising SDKs
