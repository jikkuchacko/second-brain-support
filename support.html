Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add new tasks?

Users can browse the built-in task library and add tasks to their list.

How do recurring tasks work?

Recurring tasks automatically appear again based on their schedule so you don't forget important routines.

Is my data stored online?

No. All tasks are stored locally on your device. The app does not collect or transmit personal data.
